EDUCATION

The Daycare Center provides supervision and care of young children during the daytime. They give a safe and nurturing environment for children.

Requirements for Enrollment:

  • The enrollee should be atleast four (4) years old.
  • Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
  • Health Record/ Immunization Card (Photocopy)

Kindergarten is one year of early education to prepare children for their elementary schooling years.

Requirements for Enrollment:

  • ECCD - Early Childhood Care and Development (Original Copy)
  • Daycare Report Card (Original Copy)
  • Birth Certificate (Original Copy)

Elementary Education aims to provide basic education knowledge and to develop the foundation skills, attitudes, and values essential to the child's personal development.

Requirements for Enrollment:

  • SF10/FORM 137
  • SF9 Report Card (Original Copy)
  • Birth Certificate (Photocopy)

Requirements for Transferees:

  • Permanent Record
  • Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
  • Good Moral
