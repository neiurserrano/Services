BARANGAY CLEARANCE

A barangay clearance certifies that a person is of good moral character and a law-abiding citizen in the community.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Fee
  • Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)

Barangay Clearance Fee
  • For Business Establishment: A fee required for obtaining clearance to operate a business within the barangay.
  • Annually for Lending Institution: A yearly fee for lending institutions to maintain their clearance.
  • Annually for Tractor Harvester: A yearly fee for tractor harvester operators to maintain their clearance.

BARANGAY CERTIFICATE

A barangay clearance verifies your good standing in the community.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Fee - 75 Pesos
  • Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)
Barangay Certification Fee
  • Certificate of Indigency: A fee for obtaining a certificate that verifies an individual's indigent status.
  • To File Action: A fee required to obtain a certification necessary for filing legal actions.

BARANGAY PERMIT

A Barangay Permit certifies that the business is allowed to operate within its jurisdiction.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Fee - An annual fee required for obtaining various permits within the barangay.
  • Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)

BARANGAY CERTIFICATE OF INDIGENCY

This certificate serves as official proof that a person or family is financially incapable of affording certain necessities

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Fee
  • Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)
